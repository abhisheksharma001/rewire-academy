Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

🛡️ Our Promise: Zero-Risk Enrollment

We are confident in the quality of our programs. If you're not satisfied, we offer a full refund within 7 days of program start — no questions asked. No hoops, no hard feelings.

1. Refund Eligibility

Time Period Refund Amount Conditions
Within 7 days of program start 100% No questions asked
8–14 days after program start 50% Must have attended at least 2 sessions
After 14 days 0% No refund applicable
Free Mastermind N/A No fees charged, so no refund applicable

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, you must:

  • Contact us via email at Hi@rewirebharat.com or request WhatsApp support
  • Provide your registered email address and program name
  • Submit the request within the eligible time period
  • Include your UPI ID or bank account details for refund transfer

3. Refund Processing

  • Refund requests are processed within 5 business days of approval
  • Funds are transferred to the original payment method or provided UPI/bank account
  • EMI cancellations are coordinated with our payment partner (Razorpay) and may take 10-14 business days to reflect
  • GST component is refunded proportionally with the program fee

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refund:

  • Third-party tool subscriptions purchased during the program
  • Certificate processing fees (if applicable)
  • Corporate bulk enrollment fees (covered under separate agreement)

5. Program Transfers

If you cannot attend your enrolled batch, you may request a one-time transfer to a future batch at no additional cost, provided the request is made at least 7 days before the current batch start date. Batch transfers are subject to seat availability.

6. Program Cancellation by Rewire

In the rare event that we cancel a program:

  • You will receive a full refund of all fees paid
  • Alternatively, you may transfer to a future batch with priority seating
  • We will notify you at least 7 days in advance via WhatsApp and email

7. Special Circumstances

We understand that life happens. In cases of:

  • Medical emergencies (with valid documentation)
  • Family emergencies
  • Job relocation to a different city

We may, at our sole discretion, offer extended refund eligibility or batch transfer options beyond the standard policy.

8. Contact for Refunds

For refund-related queries, contact us:

  • Email: Hi@rewirebharat.com
  • Response time: Within 48 hours on email

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on our website. Refund requests are evaluated based on the policy in effect at the time of your enrollment.
