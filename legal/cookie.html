This Cookie Policy explains how Rewire Education Pvt. Ltd. (“Rewire Academy”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, understand how you interact with content, and improve your experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly (e.g., form submissions, security).
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it.
  • Marketing cookies: Used to measure the effectiveness of our advertising and deliver relevant messages.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party services (such as Google Analytics and advertising platforms) that place cookies on your device. These services have their own privacy and cookie policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on this page.
